How it works

1. Create an occasion page

Add the recipient's name and a deadline.

2. Share the link

Via email or social networks with anyone who may want to contribute.

3. Choose your group card design & how to spend the money!

When the deadline hits, you’ll have a pot of money ready to spend and a collection of messages for a greetings card.

Simply choose your greetings card design and we will pop it in the post.

You can also choose how to spend the money pot:

One of our gifts

Gift voucher

UK bank transfer

It's up to you
